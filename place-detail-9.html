A Journey into the Wilderness

T.K. Colony, officially known as the Thannikkadavu Eco-Tourism project, is an emerging destination for those who want to experience the raw beauty of Nilambur's forests. Located on the banks of the Karimpuzha river, it offers a perfect blend of water-based activities and forest exploration. The area is known for its biodiversity and is a frequent spot for wild elephant sightings, especially during early mornings and evenings.



Key Highlights

  • Bamboo rafting along the Karimpuzha river
  • Wildlife watchtowers for elephant viewing
  • Jungle trekking with experienced tribal guides
  • Beautiful riverbank picnic spots
  • Insights into local tribal life and culture
