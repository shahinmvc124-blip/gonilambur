Conolly's Plot

Vadapuram, Nilambur

SHOP PLACES TO VISIT PLACES TO STAY RESTAURANTS FUEL STATIONS

Discover its Beauty

Conolly's Plot is the world's oldest teak plantation, named after H.V. Conolly, the Malabar District Collector during the British rule. It stands as a testament to the first forestry efforts in India and houses trees over 150 years old.



Key Highlights

  • World's oldest teak plantation
  • Hanging bridge over Chaliyar river
  • Giant 'Kannimari' Teak tree
  • Peaceful nature walk
  • Rich colonial history
