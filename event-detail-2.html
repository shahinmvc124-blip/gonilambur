Monsoon Rain Walk

About the Event

The Monsoon Rain Walk is a unique initiative by the eco-tourism department to celebrate the arrival of the Kerala monsoons. Participants will be guided through the dense Nedumkayam rainforest, crossing small streams and witnessing the forest come alive. The package includes traditional Kerala snacks, rainforest education, and a refreshing dip in the natural pools. It's an immersive experience for nature lovers and photographers.

Event Highlights

  • Expert guided sessions and cultural immersion.
  • Opportunity to interact with local artisans and communities.
  • Scenic locations and comfortable arrangements.
  • Traditional refreshments and local snacks provided.
