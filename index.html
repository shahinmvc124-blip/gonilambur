Welcome to Nilambur

Nilambur is not just a town; it is a story written in teak leaves and river songs. From its royal heritage and historic plantations to its modern eco-tourism initiatives, Nilambur stands as a perfect blend of history and nature. The Chaliyar River flows through its heart, carrying centuries of memories, while the forests whisper stories of strength and beauty.
